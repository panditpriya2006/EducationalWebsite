Who is Malala?

Malala is a brave young girl who retaliated against the banning of female education in her country, even after being shot and put in a coma.

What is the purpose of this website?

Our website aims to help people all over the world with education. We are an easy place to find links that will help you in several different subjects

Are we officially associated with Malala?

No, but we stand by the same values as her. Her bravery inspired us to dedicate our website to her cause.
